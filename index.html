My Padel Journey

A day at a padel tournament starts early with a good breakfast, followed by a warm-up at around 8am. Matches start around 9 a.m. and continue until the end of the day. Between matches, I take breaks to eat and rest. After the matches, I focus on recovery, taking care of my body with stretching and the right diet. Finally, I end the day by analyzing my performance to improve for the next day..
